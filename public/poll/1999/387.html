Garmarna and Hedningarna are from Sweden and meld traditional Swedish and Finnish music with a modern rock/electronic aesthetic. (www.noside.com)

The Pilgrims can be explored at www.thepilgrims.com

The Japanese New Music Festival album on Magaibutsu features five related performers: Zubizuva, Akaten, Atsushi Tsuyama, Tatsuya Yoshida, and Ruins.
