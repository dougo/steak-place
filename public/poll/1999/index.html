Chugchanga-L Members' Favorite Records of 1999

The eligibility rules changed a bit this year: anything officially released in (or near) 1999 was allowed, including reissues. As you can see, it didn't really affect the results that much (probably because there's not much consensus among all the reissues)-- only one reissue cracked the top 20 (Belle & Sebastian)-- but this way I didn't have to worry about making special exceptions or convoluting the rules wording to make sense.
