In no particular order:

Moloko: I AM NOT A DOCTOR (the Echo label, UK)

Really intriguing danceable stuff. Not run of the mill at all. Worth every penny of the hefty import price.

Pilote: ANTENNA (certificate eighteen, UK)

An intriguingly wierd record. Not quite drum'n'bass, not quite noise, not quite anything but an isolationist playing with crappy old sounds. Also worth the import price.

Beck: MIDNITE VULTURES (DGC)

Um, what can I say? It's the Prince record Prince never recorded. Play it on a date, get laid that night.

Boredoms: VISION CREATION NEWSUN (WEA Japan)

Yes, I bought the import. In the croaking box. I am insane.
