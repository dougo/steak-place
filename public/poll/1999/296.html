At the top: The Magnetic Fields is probably my favorite album of the decade, if anyone cares. I never knew I was such a sucker for show tunes. It ain't easy, you try it.

On the Bottom: The Sleater-Kinney is either brilliant or slight. I'll let you know the first time I make it through the lyric sheet.
