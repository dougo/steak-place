Don't Call It A Comeback... Well, All right, Call It A Comeback:  Jon Easdale
(the singer/songwriter from Dramarama) was the nicest surprise of the year, a
terrific CD full of biting, sardonic tunes with hooks aplenty.  Also in the
comeback department was the new CD from Joe King and the reconstituted Queers;
fresh out of rehab, with a new label and band, Joe used his battle with drugs
and alcohol as the inspiration for clever, catchy, funny  punk tunes, and they
were the most entertaining live band I saw all year (caught them five times in
'98, on both coasts!)
