Doug--

In considering exactly *why* it took me so long to get this together for
you, I realized that my lack of inspiration is directly related to my
decreased interest in "today's music," (i.e., specifically dance- or
beat-oriented electronic music), coupled with my increased interest in older
(pre-1975) folk/psych/trad.-type stuff.  It wouldn't have been difficult to get
together a list of 50 or so pre-1975 rekkids that rocked my world; as far
as I'm concerned, the best record I heard all year was Fraser & DeBolt's
_With Ian Guenther_.  Thing is, that album was released in 1970.  On
Columbia.  

That said, insofar as "new music" goes, I give you...
