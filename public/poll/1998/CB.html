My Top Twenty (or so) Favorite Recordings of 1998

Well, I put out the first two records, P.E.E.'s "The Roaring Mechanism" and
Virginia Dare's "Baby Got Away", on my own label (Absolutely Kosher) this
year and despite permission in the "rules" of this poll, they are too close
to my heart to put on this list.  That being said, I have no qualms about
placing them beside any other record on the list.

1. Cat Power – _MoonPix_ CD/LP (Matador)
2. Curious Digit – _Hessian Hills_  CD (Jagjaguwar)
3. Neutral Milk Hotel – _In the Aeroplane Over the Sea_ CD (Merge)
4. And You Will Know Us By The Trail Of Dead – self-titled CD (Trance
Syndicate)
5. Mark Hollis – self-titled CD (Polydor)
6. Sue Garner – _To Run More Smoothly_ CD (Thrill Jockey)
7. Various Artists – _Zum Audio Vol.2_ CD (Zum)
8. Mogwai – _Kicking A Dead Pig / Mogwai Fears Satan Remixes_ CD (Jetset)
9. Aislers Set – _Terrible Things Happen_ CD (Slumberland)
10. Melt-Banana – _"Charlie"_ CD (Project A-Zap)
11. L'Augmentation - _"Soleil"_ 7" & Pop-Off Tuesday - _"Unworldly"_ 7"
(both on Pickled Egg)
12. Yume Bitsu - _Giant Surface Music Falling to Earth like Jewels from the
Sky_ CD (Ba Da Bing!)
13. Dynamic Truths - _"You Take It All"_ 7" (Merge)
14. Quasi - _Featuring "Birds"_ CD (Up)
15. I Am Spoonbender - _Sender/Receiver_ CD (GSL)
16. Beatnik Filmstars - _Boss Disque_ CD (Merge)
17. Cradle Of Filth - _Cruelty and the Beast_ CD (Fierce/Mayhem)
18. Zoobombs - _Welcome Back, Zoobombs!_ CD(Emperor Norton)
19. Los Amigos Invisibles - _The New Sound Of The Venezuelan Gozadera_ CD
(Luaka Bop)
20. The Union of a Man and a Woman - _The Sound of the Union of a Man and a
Woman_ CD (Jagjaguwar)
21.  Jessica Bailiff - _Even In Silence_ CD (Kranky)
22. Come - _Gently, Down The Stream_ CD (Matador)

a fictional mix tape (like last year) may turn up along with more
descriptive info on the 22, but I just wanted to stand up and be counted.
