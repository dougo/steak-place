Carl Youngdahl's Chugchanga-L Poll 2000 Entry

Here are good records from 2000 (or so -- I am not so sure about
the Finnish ones, though I know they take while to become available). I
didn't hear too much this year, but these records I know well and find quite
good.

Kirvasto - Taydellisia Kysymyksia, Taydellisia Vastauksia (Bad Vugum)
Circle - Pori (Feldspar)
Circle - Prospekt (Ektroverde)
University of Errors - Money Doesn't Make It (innerSPACE)
Har Mar Superstar - Har Mar Superstar (Kill Rock Stars)
Oxes - Money Pyramid (Monitor)
Sean Na Na - Return of the Unicorn (Troubleman Unlimited)
Teoremi - I Teoremi (Akarma)

The Teoremi CD and the "Pori" CD by Circle are reissues.
