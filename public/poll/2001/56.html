The above records were chosen because (with the exception of the Ride box)
they are impeccable documents of the more-or-less current activities of
extant artists. Following is a list of other notable records released this
year, including a separate section for straight reissues or records
created from archival material. My reasoning for excluding this later
category from the main list is twofold: 1) Repeat licensing and
repackaging has made it very difficult (and to a certain extent,
undesirable) to track these types of releases too closely and 2) I think
the focus of year-end best of lists should remain on living, breathing
artists who can benefit from your attention.
