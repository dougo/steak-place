Dave Reid's Favorite Records Of 2002

all re-releases for me this year...

  1. Various, Studio One Story (Soul Jazz )
    1. retrospective telling the story of jamaica's studio one, amazing.
  2. Milton Nascimento, Travessia (Dubas Brazil)
    2. milton nascimento's first album, incredibly gorgeous (and incredibly layered) post-bossa.
  3. Lo Borges, Lo Borges (1972) (EMI Odeon)
    3. solo album from nasicimento's guitarist and collaborator in the "clube da esquina", again gorgeous, this time from a more pop-rock perspective.
