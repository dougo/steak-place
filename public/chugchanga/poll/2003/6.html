scott colburn's Favorite Records of 2003

  1. Leviathan 10th Level of Suicide (Moribund)
    black metal that burns when you touch it
  2. Decapitato Blacked (Headless)
    ex die keuzen singer rearing his head again. It's like Big Black with Ozzy Osbourne singing
  3. Cerberus Shoal Chaiming the Knoblessone (North East Indie)
    yeah, so what if I recorded it, I love it. Beefheart meets flaming lips meets butthole surfers meets art.
  4. Minor Threat First Demo Tape (Dischord)
    kings for the new day
  5. Frank Zappa Halloween (Vaulternative)
    probably the best live concert surround live mix I've ever heard, and I told Joe that to his face.
  6. Faith and Disease Passport to Kumming (Projeckt)
    (See Cerberus Shoal) but it _really is_ like Joy Division.
  7. Die Lawn Chocolate Retarder (Anthemic)
    Ex Leotard Geeks and Wheezing Dogz. The title says it all, but it's totally strange retardation. Makes no sense at all.
  8. Polyphonic Spree the Beginning Stages (Good)
    especially like the last half of the record. This track clocks in at 30 minutes. Brilliant!
