Anonymous Chugchanga-L Member #3's Favorite Records of 2003

The following records are in no particular order.

  1. Constantines Shine a Light (Sub Pop)
  2. Pretty Girls Make Graves The New Romance (Matador)
    If I had the ability to reprogram every mall-punks mental definition of "punk rock" music, I would use this during the process.
  3. The Rapture Echoes (Universal)
    Not sure this record will matter at all in 6 months, but it's got something that keeps me listening right now (aside from the blatant Cure ripoffs).
  4. TV on the Radio Young Liars (Touch and Go)
    The Pixies cover was enough to cement this record in my top of '03. Everything beyond that is just gravy on the mashed potatoes.
  5. The Mars Volta De-Loused in the Comatorium (Univeral)
    Jon Theodore makes this record. Remove him and it's just a pile prog-noodling.
