Nuuj's Favorite Records of 2003

These are not in order. I tried a bit to get my true favorites into the top 20, but I failed. But as I don't care what the final Poll is, I'll just let it go like this. I'm sure I'm forgetting a bunch of stuff.

  1. grand ulena gatewway to dignity
    Best Free Jazz record by a math rock band. The EP was also good, but no where near this good.
  2. Melt Banana Cell-Scape
    Their best album ever. The songs are really catchy. Guitar playing and effect use is beyond belief.
  3. Laddio Bolocko The Life and Times of
    Too bad great bands have to break up before anyone notices them.
  4. Cheer-Accident Introducing Lemon
    Their best in a long time. Gumballhead the cat is also good.
  5. deerhoof apple o'
    I like this better than Reville, but perhaps that's because I heard it first.
  6. need new body ufo
    This is great weird Pop record.
  7. Xerophonics Copying Machine Music
    As someone who works in the development of copiers, this concept has been on my to do list for years. No I don't have to bother. Catures everyting that is great about the sounds copiers make.
  8. pelican pelican (Hydrahead)
    Best Bitch Magnet record in a long time. Too bad the album sucks ass.
  9. loincloth Church Burntings b/w New Jersey (Southern Lord)
    Someone should give these guys a boat load of money and make them write and record full time. Head and shoulders above most metal.
  10. harvey milk the singles (Relapse)
    Not their best, but as I only discoverd their other amazing albums this year, I feel I should include this. Some weak spots, but also some amazing moments.
  11. ui answers (Matador)
    Better than liquid liquid.
  12. Pinebender The high price of living too long with a single dream
    Why aren't these guys huge? Best Indie-Pop record of 2003. Remind me of Buffalo bands Milf and Tugboat Annie.
  13. phantomsmasher & venetian snares split 7 inch
    The Phantomsmasher album of 2002, is my favorite of that year. This follows as logical extension of those ideas. The venetian Snares album of 2003 is also pretty great.
  14. The Whip freelance liaison b/w sheep and goat judgment
    I plaid this to death all summer long. I hope the remaning members do something with all those other songs I heard on a live bootleg.
  15. valina vagabond
    Great Shellac style math rock from Austria. Why doesn't T&g put this out instead of that bullshit dance crap.
  16. the necks drive by
    They claim they play jazz, but it sounds like post rock to me. Only it doesn't suck, the way most post rock does. Hypnotic
  17. Hospitals S/T (In The Red)
    Best rock and roll record in years!
  18. Quintron Are You Ready For An Organ Solo?
    Wow. I've always had a soft spot for Quintron, but never has his genius been so well produced and packaged.
  19. Animal Collective Here Comes The Indian
  20. wolf eyes mugger (hanson)
  21. Yo La Tengo Summer Sun (Matador)
    I'm one of the few people who like this better than the EP. I don't care if they never play rock music again.
  22. sunn 0))) white 1
    More Preston please.
  23. sicbay overraction time
    Much better than Collosomite.
  24. negura bunget 'n crugu bradului
    Weird ass Black metal with great production values.
  25. the screamin' mee-mees live from the basement 1975-1997
    I want to drive a tractor like Max Factor.
  26. Bardo Pond On the Ellipse
  27. Usaisamonster Tasheyana Compost
  28. 3 second kiss music out of music
    Best US Maple Record of 2003
  29. sightings absolutes
    Much better than their first.
  30. ludacris chicken and beer
    Not as good as Word of Mouf, but there are still alot of hits on this.
  31. 400 blows the sore thumb 7 inch
    Sounds sort of like the early years of my band Hilkka. Only it's well produced and really good.
  32. black dice cone toaster
    Catchy as fuck.
  33. halo body of light
    Wonderful drum sounds. One of the heaviest releases of 2003.
  34. khanate things viral
    Not as good as their last
  35. joan of arc so much staying alive and lovelessness
    They should of combined the best parts of this, with the other album and made one great album instead of one and a quarter good ones.
  36. Viki / Hair Police split (Load/SNSE)
  37. melted men 7'' ep (dead mind-nerve rust)
  38. James Chance Box set
  39. 400 Blows black rainbow
  40. cedric 'im' brooks & the light of saba the light of saba

2003 Disappointments: Pelican - Australasia, US Maple - Purple on Time, Earth - Sunn Amps and Smashed Guitars Live, An Albatross - We Are the Lazer Vikings, Lightning bolt - wonderful rainbow, Total Shutdown, Flying Luttenbahcers, Pink and Brown, the fall - the real new fall lp (formerly country on the click) 2003 Shit List: TV On The Radio - s/t, !!! - Me & Giuliani Down By The Schoolyard
