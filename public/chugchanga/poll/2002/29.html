Anonymous Chugchanga Member #5's Favorite Records Of 2002

  1. Tracy + the Plastics, Forever Sucks EP (Chainsaw)
  2. Yeah Yeah Yeahs, self-titled EP (Touch and Go)
  3. David Bowie, Heathen (Sony)
    His best since Young Americans
  4. Johnny Cash, American IV: The Man Comes Around (Universal)
    Although most of the songs are covers, they are still done by the Man in Black.
  5. Interpol, Turn On the Bright Lights (Matador)
  6. Sonic Youth, Murray Street (Geffen)
  7. Pavement, Slanted and Enchanted (Remastered) (Matador)
    There is so much more new material that this almost counts as a new release.
  8. The Liars, They Threw Us All In A Trench And Stuck A Monument On Top (Mute)
