Loud Stuff (1993)

A friend of mine wanted to hear some stuff I liked, and he said he liked stuff that was loud, so I put together a bunch of stuff he hadn't heard before that I thought he might like. There's not really much rhyme or reason to this collection, but I liked the way it goes together.
