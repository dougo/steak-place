[2 & 3 and 6 & 7 are ties --dougo]

  • PARTS UNKNOWN -- an excellent Toronto band in the vein of Wire, The Fall, Joy Division, etc., with some modern ambient/punk reference points as well. Find 'em on the web at www.partsunknown.net
  • BEAUTY PAGEANT -- extremely witty & catchy indie pop from Chicago. email 'em at users@ripco.com
  • SOLVENT -- tuneful techno from T.O., definitely the best "IDM" (ugh) release of '99. web: www.suctionrecords.com
  • APPLIANCE -- post-rock that rocks, from England; very laconic Television-y guitars and vocals, sweet
  • CHRIS ROCK -- "the trenchcoat mafia -- what the fuck was on they minds? Oh, they was upset cuz people picked on 'em, we was the outcasts. There was six of the motherfuckers! I didn't have six friends in high school. I don't have six friends now!"
