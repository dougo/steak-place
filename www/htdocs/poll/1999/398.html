I don't think it's legit for me to vote for it per se, but the Don Cab singles comp woulda been on here somewhere were it not for the obvious conflict.

I find myself spending more time on music of the past than present these days, though in compiling this list I'm led to think that perhaps things aren't so bad right now after all. (Plus, it can't account for things like the Ex and Magma live, to just note stuff off the top of my head.)
