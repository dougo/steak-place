Chugchanga-L Members' Favorite Records of 1997

Same rules as last year: subscribers were asked to send me lists of up to 20 of their favorite records released in 1997 (or late 1996), of any length and in any format. Compilations and box sets were allowed, but reissues and bootlegs were not. (A special exception was made for the remixed reissue of Raw Power, which got 4 votes.) Here's the collected top 29:
