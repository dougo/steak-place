Chugchanga-L Members' Favorite Records of 2001

The eligibility rules were the same as the last two years: anything officially released in (or near) 2001 was allowed, including reissues. The order of voters' top 20 lists did not matter, except---new for this year---voters could optionally designate one top record, which would be used for breaking ties (like last year's tie for number 1).
