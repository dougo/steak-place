Kathy Fennessy's Chugchanga-L Poll 2001 Entry

My Top 10:

1. White Stripes - White Blood Cells (Sympathy for the Record Industry)
2. LiLiPUT/Kleenex - The Complete Recordings (Kill Rock Stars)
3. The Shins - Oh, Inverted World (Sub Pop)
4. Stereolab - Sound-Dust (Elektra)
5. Nuggets II: Original Artyfacts from the British Empire and Beyond (Rhino)
6. The Beta Band - Hot Shots II (Astralwerks)
7. Wipers Box Set (Zeno Records)
8. Le Tigre - Feminist Sweepstakes (Mr. Lady)
9. Clinic - Internal Wrangler (Domino)
10. Built to Spill - Ancient Melodies of the Future (Warner Bros.)

A fair amount of old stuff as usual. Although Air made my list (twice) last
year, 10,000 Hz Legend failed to float my boat. Wasn't too knocked out by
those Radiohead recordings--Amnesiac & I Might Be Wrong: Live
Recordings--either. Honorable mention props to Bjork, Outkast, & Dungeon
Family.
