Chugchanga-L Members' Favorite Records of 1996

This year, the rules were slightly different: subscribers were asked to send me lists of up to 20 of their favorite records released in 1996 (or late 1995), of any length and in any format. Compilations and box sets were allowed, but reissues and bootlegs were not. Here's the collected top 20:
