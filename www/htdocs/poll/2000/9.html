Josh Davis's Chugchanga-L Poll 2000 Entry

In no particular order, here's my 2000 list (sorry, there are a few on major
labels, but after all, they are PJ Harvey, Sonic Youth and Johnny Cash):

- Deltron, "3030", Ark75
- White Stripes, "De Stijl", Sympathy for the Record Industry
- Harvey PJ, "Stories from the City, Stories from the Sea", Island
- Giant Sand, "Chore of Enchantment", Thrill Jockey
- Johnny Cash, "American III: Solitary Man", Sony/Columbia
- Smog, "Dongs of Sevotion", Drag City
- Jurassic 5, "Quality Control", Interscope
- Dirty Three, "Whatever You Love, You Are", Touch & Go
- Godspeed You Black Emperor, "Lift Your Skinny Fists Like Antennas To Heaven", Kranky
- Adams Ryan, "Heartbreaker", Bloodshot
- Sonic Youth, "NYC Flowers and Ghosts", Geffen
- Various Artists, "O Brother Where Art Thou?", Mercury Nashville
- Buckner Richard, "The Hill", Overcoat
