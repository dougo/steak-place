Craig Hutler's Chugchanga-L Poll 2000 Entry

Music that made me happy in 2000 (in no particular order):

Silkworm - Lifestyle (and live at the Empty Bottle)
High Dependency Unit - Memento Mori (and one hell of a live show)
Storm & Stress - Under Thunder and Fluorescent Light
Shellac - 1000 Hurts
Radiohead - Kid A
Sigur Ros - Aegatis Byrjun
Lassie Foundation - Pacifico
The Dictators live at the Empty Bottle
Mirror America live at the Beat Kitchen (John Lee of aMiniture fame, Noah L.
of Hurl fame, and Andy Cohen of Silkworm, and the bass player of the day)

To be honest the majority of music I bought in 2000 wasn't made/released
in 1990 let alone 2000 but all of the above items made my year much more
enjoyable.
