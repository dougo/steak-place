Chugchanga-L Members' Favorite Records of 1995

Subscribers to Chugchanga-L were asked to send me lists of their favorite full-length and non-full-length records released in 1995, up to ten of each, including new collections of previously released material but excluding bootlegs. Here's the executive summary:
  • Full-length records:

    1. Yo La Tengo, Electr-o-pura (Matador) - 16 votes
    2. Guided by Voices, Alien Lanes (Matador) - 13 votes
    2. Six Finger Satellite, Severe Exposure (Sub Pop) - 13 votes
    4. Don Caballero, Don Caballero 2 (Touch & Go) - 10 votes
    4. Unwound, The Future Of What (Kill Rock Stars) - 10 votes
    6. Pavement, Wowee Zowee (Matador) - 9 votes
    7. Fugazi, Red Medicine (Dischord) - 8 votes
    8. Palace Music, Viva Last Blues (Drag City) - 7 votes
    8. Sleater-Kinney, Sleater-Kinney (Chainsaw) - 7 votes
    8. Various Artists, Kids - Original Motion Picture Soundtrack (London) - 7 votes

  • Non-full-length records:

    1. Grifters, Eureka EP (Shangri-La) - 9 votes
    2. The For Carnation, Fight Songs EP (Matador) - 8 votes
    2. Tortoise, Gamera/Cliff Dweller Society 12" (Duophonic) - 8 votes
    4. Uzeda, 4 EP (Touch & Go) - 6 votes
    5. Cibo Matto, ``Birthday Cake'' b/w ``Black Hole Sun'' (El Diablo) - 5 votes
    5. Gastr Del Sol, Harp Factory on Lake Street EP (Table of the Elements) - 5 votes
    5. godheadSilo, Elephantitus of the Night EP (Kill Rock Stars) - 5 votes
