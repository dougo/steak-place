Craig Hutler's Favorite Records of 2003

  1. Calla Televise (Arena Rock)
    Enjoyed both live and on CD.
  2. Yeah Yeah Yeahs Fever to Tell (Interscope)
    I'm not a big fan of the first EP but I have to say this record won me over.
  3. The Notwist Neon Golden (Domino)
  4. Outkast Speakerboxxx / The Love Below (Arista)
  5. Richard Hawley Lowedges (XL)
  6. Sloan Action Pact (BMG)
    Guitar pop like they made it when I was in middle school. Not their best record but still hyper enjoyable.
  7. The Action Rolled Gold (Reaction)
  8. British Sea Power The Decline of British Sea Power (Rough Trade)
  9. The Who The Kids Are Alright - DVD (Pioneer)
    A Fantastic reissue of an already fantastic movie.
  10. The Clash The Essential Clash - DVD (Sony)
  11. Joe Strummer & the Mescaleros Streetcore (Hellcat)
    A record that left me with the feeling that Strummer's work was only going to get stronger.
  12. Television Marquee Moon (Rhino)
  13. Prefuse 73 One Word Extinguisher (Warp)
  14. Seam Live in Milwaukee @ the Mad Planet
    Inspired.
  15. Paul Weller Live @ the Vic
