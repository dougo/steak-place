Iikka Korhonen's Favorite Records of 2003

OK, here are my favourite records of 2003. In alpahabetical order. I know that this list would look very different a month from now, but that's the way it goes...

  1. Baby Woodrose Money for Soul (Bad Afro)
  2. Coleman, King It's Dance Time! (Norton)
  3. Comets on Fire Comets on Fire (Alternative Tentacles)
    I know the original vinyl version came out earlier, but I didn't hear it and this one has several extra tracks, so..
  4. Cosmo Jones Beat Machine Negrospiritualized (Hot Igloo)
    Finnish rhythm madness, Hot Igloo used to be (half of) Bad Vugum, if that matters.
  5. Crack Pipes Snakes in My Veins (Emperor Jones)
    Not a perfect record, but some excellent songs..
  6. Cramps Fiends of Dope Island (Vengeance)
    I didn't really expect anything from them at this point.
  7. Dirtbombs Dangerous Magical Noise (In the Red)
  8. Erickson, Roky and the Aliens Don't Knock the Rok! (Norton)
    Great FUN!
  9. Flaming Sideburns Sky Pilots (Ranch)
  10. Fleshtones Do You Swing? (Yeproc)
    Still going strong!
  11. Guided by Voices Hardcore UFOs (Matador)
    It is impossible to follow R.Pollard's output, but this comp is GREAT! Dozens of brilliant pop songs.
  12. Kings of Leon Youth & Young Manhood (RCA)
  13. Little Killers Little Killers (Crypt)
    The best rock'n'roll record of 2003
  14. Superbees High Volume (Acetate)
    This is R-O-C-K
  15. Television Live at the Old Waldorf (Rhino)
  16. White Stripes Elephant (XL)
    Seven Nation Army is the best rock song of 2003
  17. Young, Neil Greendale (Reprise)
  18. Various artists Twenty Years of Dischord (Dischord)
  19. Various artists Sympathetic Sounds of Toe-Rag Studios (Sympathy for the Record Industry)
  20. Various artists Great Googa Mooga! (Ace)
    These words don't make any sense!
